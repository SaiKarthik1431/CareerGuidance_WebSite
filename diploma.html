DIPLOMA COURSES

Importance and uses of Diploma Courses:

  • Specialized Skill Development:

    1. Diploma courses offer specialized training, allowing students to acquire practical skills in a specific field.
    2. These skills are directly applicable to various industries, making graduates job-ready.

  • Quick Entry to the Workforce:

    1. Diploma programs are typically shorter in duration compared to traditional degrees.
    2. Graduates can enter the workforce faster, which is advantageous for those seeking immediate employment opportunities.

  • Cost-Effective Education:

    1. Diploma courses are often more affordable than bachelor's or master's degrees.
    2. They provide a cost-effective way to gain valuable skills and qualifications.

  • Industry-Relevant Curriculum:

    1. Diploma programs are designed in consultation with industry experts.
    2. This ensures that students learn up-to-date and relevant knowledge and practices.

  • High Demand in Technical Fields:

    1. Diploma courses are highly sought after in technical fields like engineering, IT, and healthcare.
    2. Graduates in these areas are in demand due to their specialized skills.

Diploma Cources:

Diploma courses in Polytechnic:

Diploma in Engineering(Polytechnic) is one among the career options where students can get Diploma in Engineering. There are various branches in Polytechnic which students can opt. The various branches in Polytechnic(Andhra Pradesh) are:

  • 1. Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
  • 2. Diploma in Computer Science Engineering
  • 3. Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • 4. Diploma in Electrical and Electronics
  • 5. Diploma in Civil Engineering
  • 6. Diploma in Mining Engineering

ELIGIBILITY:

A candidate must have passed SSC with an aggregate of 50% of marks He/she must qualify Polycet Entrance examination conducted by the Department of Technical Education.

Scheme of Examination:

  1. The examination is conducted for 120 marks where 120 bits will be given.
  2. There is no negative marking.
  3. The weightage is as follows
    • Mathematics - 60 Marks
    • Physics- 30 Marks
    • Chemistry -30 Marks

How to apply:

The department of technical education generally releases Polycet notification in the month of April.For more details students can visit www.polycetap.nic.in
