NATIONAL DEFENCE ACADEMY

Importance and uses of National Defence Academy:

  • Early Entry to the Armed Forces:

    1. NDA allows candidates to start their journey towards becoming officers in the Indian Army, Navy, or Air Force right after the 10th class.
    2. It provides an early entry point into the armed forces, which is not possible through traditional civilian education.

  • Camaraderie and Networking:

    1. Cadets build strong bonds with peers and instructors.
    2. These connections last throughout their military careers, providing a support system.

  • Financial Benefits:

    1. Cadets receive a stipend during their training, which increases as they progress.
    2. After completing NDA, they start earning a military salary with various allowances.

  • National Service:

    1. Joining NDA is a path to serve the nation with honor and commitment.
    2. Graduates are prepared to protect their country and its interests.

  • Respected Profession:

    1. A career as an officer in the armed forces is highly respected and admired.
    2. NDA graduates are considered national heroes and role models.

ELIGIBILITY:

***A candidate must have passed SSC with an aggregate of 50% of marks. He/she must qualify National Defence Academy Entrance*** Examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC).
