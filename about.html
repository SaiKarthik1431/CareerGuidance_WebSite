ABOUT CAREER GUIDANCE


YOU MAY THINK WHY DO WE NEED CAREER GUIDANCE AND WHAT OUR PROJECT IS ABOUT ! ! HERE ARE YOUR ANSWERS:

Q.1 Why is career counseling important in schools?

Ans. Career counseling is critical to making students aware of their interests and abilities at the beginning of their careers. If students get counseling during schooling, they would be able to identify their professional interests and opt for a suitable education path.


Q.2 What are the benefits of career guidance?

Ans. Career guidance has the following benefits: It helps clarify your values, interests, personality, and skills. Career guidance is an effective way to let you know the available options suitable for you. It helps you explore your interest and abilities on a specific career path. Complete career guidance also educates you on the labor market, industries, and job types. It assists you in making an informed and content decision about your career.


Q.3 Who needs career counseling?

Ans. Every student or working professional needs career counseling on defining moments in their lives. School students need career guidance when choosing their stream and subjects or migrating to college, and college students need it when they are confused about their careers. Professionals need it when they want to make a shift in their professions. People facing hard times and struggling with their careers also need career guidance.


Q.4 Why do people go to career counseling?

Ans. People go to career counseling to filter their interests and make sound career decisions. They want/need to identify their interest field and discover if they're suitable for it or not. They also undergo counseling when unsure about their current job or work domain.


Q.5 Is career Counselling helpful?

Ans. Yes, it is helpful because career counseling doesn't only present you with the job market and opportunities but also discovers your interest and abilities. Career counseling helps you identify the domain in which you're interested and can do good.
